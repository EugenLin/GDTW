關於Google 安全瀏覽服務 - About Google Safe Browsing

本站使用「Google 安全瀏覽服務」檢查以下潛在風險：
1. 社交工程
2. 惡意軟體

本站依據「Google 安全瀏覽服務」的檢測結果將在畫面上顯示不同圖標:
1. 「Unchecked!」: 此網址可能因為「Google 安全瀏覽服務」的每日檢測額度已滿或網路因素未成功被「Google 安全瀏覽服務」檢測
2. 「Checked!」: 此網址通過「Google 安全瀏覽服務」檢測, 屬於風險較低的原始網址
3. 「Unsafe!」: 此網址未通過「Google 安全瀏覽服務」, 此原始網址具有高度風險性, 請自行評估是否繼續前往

最後，務必理解「Google 安全瀏覽服務」所提供的保護並非完美無缺。網站可能會出現誤報（將安全網站標記為有風險），或未檢測到某些真正有風險的網站。Google 致力於提供準確且最新的資訊，但無法保證其結果完全正確。

This site checks for the following potential risks using Google Safe Browsing:
1. Social engineering
2. Malware

Based on the results from Google Safe Browsing, different icons will be displayed on the screen:
1. "Unchecked!": The URL may not have been successfully checked by Google Safe Browsing due to reaching the daily check limit or network issues.
2. "Checked!": The URL has passed Google Safe Browsing inspection and is considered a lower-risk original URL.
3. "Unsafe!": The URL has failed Google Safe Browsing checks and is deemed highly risky. Please assess carefully whether to proceed.

Finally, it's important to understand that the protection provided by Google Safe Browsing is not foolproof. There may be false positives (marking a safe website as risky) or failures to detect certain truly risky sites. Google strives to provide accurate and up-to-date information, but cannot guarantee that the results are always correct.


本站使用「Google 安全瀏覽服務V4」自動檢查目標網址的安全性，我們會依據 「Google 安全瀏覽服務V4」來檢測潛在的風險。請注意，「Google 安全瀏覽服務V4」所提供的警告並不表示該網站 100% 不安全，它僅表示該網站可能存在風險。當使用者看到警告訊息時，請理解這是「Google 安全瀏覽服務V4」基於潛在風險的判斷，而非對該網頁的絕對認定。

This site uses Google Safe Browsing V4 to automatically check the safety of target URLs. We rely on Google Safe Browsing V4 to detect potential risks. Please note that a warning from Google Safe Browsing V4 does not necessarily mean that the website is 100% unsafe; it only indicates that the site may pose a risk. When users see a warning message, please understand that it is based on Google Safe Browsing V4's assessment of potential risks, rather than an absolute determination of the webpage's safety.
